Getting Started
Simple steps to begin playing IPL26 - BFL
1
Sign in to your team account
Use the password provided by your league admin. Select your team from the dropdown on the login page. Each team has a unique password.
2
View your squad on the My Squad page
Your 12-player squad has been set by the admin. You'll see your Captain (2×), Vice-Captain (1.5×), and Impact Player (3× or 0×). These multipliers apply during point calculation.
3
Submit your prediction before the deadline
For each match, predict: (1) the target score (first innings total + 1), and (2) which team will win. You can also activate your Impact Player for a 3× multiplier — or keep them OFF to save the usage.
4
Watch the match and check your points
Once the admin enters stats and calculates points, your score appears in the leaderboard and your match breakdown. Check Analytics for a deep dive into your season performance.
🎯
How Predictions Work
Earn bonus points by predicting match outcomes
Before each match, you have a window to submit your prediction. The window closes automatically at the deadline (usually just before toss). You cannot change your prediction after it locks.
WHAT TO PREDICT
• Target score (Team A total + 1)
• Winning team
• Impact Player ON or OFF
POINTS YOU CAN EARN
• Exact target → +250
• Off by ±1 → +150
• Correct winner → +25
⚡
The Impact Player
Your most powerful per-match tool — use it wisely
Your squad has one designated Impact Player (set by the admin). Before each match, you decide whether to activate them or not.
3×
Impact ON
Player's base points are multiplied by 3. Uses 1 of your 8 seasonal activations.
0×
Impact OFF
Player scores 0 for this match. No activation used. Save it for bigger games.
⚠️ Max 8 activations per season. Abandoned matches don't count. Plan your activations around strong batting pitches and your player's form.

Squad Composition
Your team structure and constraints
🏏
Squad Size
  • Exactly 12 players per squad
  • Squad set by admin via auction
  • No transfers during the season
  • Injured players can be replaced temporarily
Overseas Limit
  • Maximum 4 overseas players
  • Same limit as IPL rules
  • Overseas players shown with badge
🎭
Role Requirements
  • Min. 2 Batters
  • Min. 3 Bowlers
  • Min. 2 All-Rounders
  • Min. 1 Wicket-Keeper
👑
Special Roles
  • Captain: 2× multiplier on all points
  • Vice-Captain: 1.5× multiplier
  • Impact Player: 3× if activated, 0× if not
  • C and VC cannot also be Impact Player
⏰
Prediction Rules
Deadlines and what happens when they pass
⏳
Deadlines
  • Deadline is typically 15 mins before match start
  • Admin can extend or reopen if needed
  • A countdown timer shows when it's closing
🔒
After Locking
  • Predictions cannot be changed
  • No new predictions accepted
  • Your last submission is final
☔
Abandoned Matches
  • No prediction points awarded
  • Squad points are also 0
  • Impact activations are refunded (don't count)
🌧
DLS Matches
  • DLS revised target is used for scoring
  • Prediction scoring applies normally
  • DLS badge shown on match cards
⚡
Impact Player Rules
✅ ALLOWED
  • → Toggle ON/OFF before deadline
  • → Different choice each match
  • → OFF = save the use for later
  • → Abandoned matches refund the use
❌ NOT ALLOWED
  • → Cannot change after deadline
  • → Captain cannot be Impact Player
  • → Vice-Captain cannot be Impact Player
  • → Only 8 total ON uses per season

Role Multipliers
Applied to squad points only — not prediction or PoM/PoT bonuses
2×
Captain
All batting, bowling, fielding points doubled. Best if your captain performs well.
1.5×
Vice-Captain
All points multiplied by 1.5. A good pick if captain fails.
3×
